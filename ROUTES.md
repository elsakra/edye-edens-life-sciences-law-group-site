## Navigation Structure

The site follows a clear hierarchical structure:

- **Header Navigation**: Our Approach, Services, Leadership, Start a Conversation (Contact)
- **Footer Navigation**: Quick Links, Services, Legal Pages
- **Service Categories**: Four main client types with detailed service offerings

## Content Mapping

All content from the original site has been preserved and organized across these routes:

- **Homepage**: Main value proposition, service overview, approach preview
- **Approach**: Detailed philosophy and core principles
- **Services**: Comprehensive breakdown by client type
- **Leadership**: Team information and expertise
- **Contact**: Multiple contact methods and FAQ

## SEO Considerations

- All pages have unique titles and meta descriptions
- Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy
- Internal linking between related pages
- Structured data for legal service business
- Mobile-responsive design

The site is designed to convert visitors through clear CTAs and multiple contact touchpoints while maintaining the professional, research-focused brand identity.
